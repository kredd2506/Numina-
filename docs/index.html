System Architecture

Seamless integration between Gmail, AWS, n8n, and QuickBooks

01

Ingestion Pipeline

Gmail triggers detect incoming financial documents. Attachments are classified and uploaded to S3, triggering Lambda-based OCR processing.

02

AI Processing

AWS Bedrock (Claude 3 Haiku) and Textract extract structured data from images and PDFs with 95%+ confidence scoring.

03

Validation & Reconciliation

n8n workflow orchestrates AI agent (Google Gemini) to classify Bills vs Invoices and validate against QuickBooks data.

04

Real-Time Sync

Validated transactions are atomically pushed to QuickBooks via HTTP API with duplicate detection and integrity guarantees.

Our Vision

We're redefining how audits are done — with intelligence, transparency, and trust. Our mission is to empower finance professionals with AI-driven insights that eliminate manual errors, reduce audit time by 80%, and ensure absolute accuracy in financial reporting.

The Story

After speaking with Edward, a Certified Public Accountant, we discovered that the core problems of CPAs and CAs persist even in the age of automation. Small-business clients without dedicated accounting teams simply shift unverified data onto their accountants, forcing them to be the end-of-chain verifiers.

Edward challenged us to build a solution that fundamentally improves the closing process. That mandate became Numina — the AI agent designed to solve the "crunch time" problem for accounting professionals globally.
